About Shoesly

Shoesly is a Ecom store for shoes. Find your favourite shoes at Shoesly. Quality, Comfort and Durability are the priorities of Shoesly. Started by Abhinav Raj Singh & Yash Chauhan, We started selling shoes only in 2022. In addition to female and male, there is a children's line. Shoes of non-dull styles and rich colors immediately catch the eye.

We are located in DTU Innovation and Incubation Foundation, Delhi, India.
We sell branded shoes at convenient prices. We focus on team-designed collaborations that appeal to our global community of global independents, creatives, and free-thinkers. We create shoes to reflect the unbound lives we live and don't stop until we get it right.

Mission

Our mission is what drives us to do everything possible to expand human potential. We do that by creating groundbreaking sport innovations, by making our products more sustainably, by building a creative and diverse global team and by making a positive impact in communities where we live and work.
